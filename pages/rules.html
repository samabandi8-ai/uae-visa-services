Visa Requirements

Essential requirements for different types of UAE visa applications

General Requirements (All Applicants)

  • Valid passport with minimum 6 months validity
  • Passport-size photographs (white background)
  • Completed visa application form
  • Proof of accommodation in UAE
  • Return flight tickets
  • Bank statements (last 3 months)
  • Travel insurance coverage

Corporate Visa Requirements

  • Company registration documents
  • Trade license copy
  • Employment contract
  • Salary certificate
  • Educational certificates
  • Experience certificates
  • Medical fitness certificate

Retired Security Officers

  • Service discharge certificate
  • Pension documents
  • Character certificate from last employer
  • Rank verification documents
  • Aadhar card copy
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Medical fitness report

Family Visa Requirements

  • Marriage certificate (for spouse)
  • Birth certificates (for children)
  • Sponsor's salary certificate
  • Accommodation proof
  • Family photographs
  • Medical insurance for family
  • Sponsor's Emirates ID copy

Document Guidelines

Important guidelines for document preparation and submission

Document Format Requirements

Digital Documents

  • PDF format preferred
  • Maximum file size: 5MB per document
  • Minimum resolution: 300 DPI
  • Color scans for colored documents

Photographs

  • Size: 35mm x 45mm
  • White background only
  • Recent (within 6 months)
  • Clear facial features

Translation Requirements

  • All documents in English or Arabic
  • Certified translations required
  • Original language copy included
  • Translator credentials attached

Attestation Requirements

  • Educational certificates attested
  • Marriage certificates legalized
  • Birth certificates authenticated
  • Commercial documents verified

Important Document Notes

Validity Requirements

All documents must be valid and not expired. Passport validity should be minimum 6 months from travel date.

Original Documents

Original documents may be required for verification. Certified copies are acceptable for submission.

Document Security

All submitted documents are handled with strict confidentiality and security protocols.
